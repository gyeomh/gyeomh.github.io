I am a first-year M.S. student in the Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence at **Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH)**, advised by Prof. [Sangwoo Mo](https://sites.google.com/view/sangwoomo/about?authuser=0).

My research interests lie in Physical AI, including Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models and multi-agent systems. I am interested in developing embodied agents capable of generalizing to unseen tasks and collaborating effectively to solve complex tasks.
